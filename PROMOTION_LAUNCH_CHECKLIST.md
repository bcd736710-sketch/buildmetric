# BuildMetric Promotion Launch Checklist

Use this before sharing the site publicly in social posts, forums, communities, directories, or outreach.

Production URL:

```text
https://buildmetriccalc.com
```

## Core Positioning

- BuildMetric is a backyard DIY calculator and material planning website for homeowners.
- Main promise: estimate materials before buying.
- Primary topics: gravel, concrete, mulch, paint, fence, shed, garden beds, and backyard chickens.
- Do not describe the site as contractor finance software, estimating services, or a paid construction business platform.

## Promotion Copy

Short version:

```text
BuildMetric is a free backyard DIY calculator site for homeowners. Estimate gravel, concrete, mulch, paint, fence, shed, garden bed, and chicken coop materials before buying.
```

Long version:

```text
BuildMetric helps homeowners plan backyard DIY projects with simple calculators and practical material guides. Use it to estimate gravel, concrete, mulch, paint, fence, shed, garden bed, and chicken coop quantities before buying supplies or comparing project options.
```

## Do Not Promote Yet As

- A paid app.
- A contractor quoting platform.
- A professional engineering tool.
- A replacement for permits, codes, product labels, or local contractor advice.
- A finished affiliate site with product recommendations.

## Weekly Promotion Routine

- Share one calculator page.
- Share one supporting guide.
- Check Google Search Console for impressions and indexed pages.
- Improve pages that receive impressions but low clicks.
- Add new content only around calculators that show early traction.

## Affiliate Readiness

- Paint, gravel, concrete slab, shed cost, and mulch calculator pages now include neutral materials checklist sections.
- These sections are ready for future affiliate links, but currently do not recommend specific retailers or products.
- Add real affiliate links only after joining a program and confirming the disclosure language is still accurate.
- Keep recommendations tied to project needs, not generic product stuffing.
